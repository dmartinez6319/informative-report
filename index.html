ENC1102 Module 3 Project

Is AI truly what it seems like?

Artificial Intellgience is becoming more mainstream and used in everyday activities, but that comes at a cost.

By Daniel Martinez March 2025

Artificial Intelligence in the Modern World

Artificial intelligence is becoming increasingly more popular and common use in the modern world since it is very accessible through a website and as a result is starting to impact and change the way we interact with the world. Artificial Intelligence has its many benefits such as increasing productivity, solving complex problems, organizing data, but all of this comes at a cost. It has many issues such as handling very private data and the many ethical or moral issues that it is being used for. In this report I will go over the many key issues around artificial intelligence and how it is impacting society in particular education institutions and how there are possible solutions and measures taken to use artificial intelligence to our advantage without the ethical or moral issues it presents.
