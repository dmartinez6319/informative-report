Artificial Intelligence in the Modern World

Artificial intelligence is becoming increasingly more popular and common use in the modern world since it is very accessible through a website and as a result is starting to impact and change the way we interact with the world. Artificial Intelligence has its many benefits such as increasing productivity, solving complex problems, organizing data, but all of this comes at a cost. It has many issues such as handling very private data and the many ethical or moral issues that it is being used for. In this report I will go over the many key issues around artificial intelligence and how it is impacting society in particular education institutions and go over some of the measures already taken to use artificial intelligence to our advantage without the ethical or moral issues that is present in it.

Privacy and Data Concerns

Artificial intelligence systems usually rely on a large amount of personal data to function effectively through answering any personalized question. This is what allows artificial intelligence to have personalization and almost instant services but at the same time it also introduces very big privacy risks. The collection, storage, and use of this private data raise concerns about unauthorized access, data breaches, and surveillance. In many cases, the data collected by artificial intelligence systems can be used in ways that individuals might not fully understand or agree to which poses a very big ethical and moral problem by the companies that create it. Through this source, The political philosophy of AI, Coeckelbergh (2022) who is an expert in this field of technology and is highly regarded says that "the extensive data collection by AI systems poses threats to individual privacy and autonomy". This shows that as artificial intelligence becomes more prevalent in many sectors of life keeping personal information safe is becoming more crucial than ever.

Misuse of Artificial Intelligence

Another issue surrounding artificial intelligence is its potential misuse. While artificial intelligence has the ability to increase productivity in many areas of life, it can also be used for harmful or cheating purposes. The risk of artificial intelligence being used to manipulate public opinion will be a big problem especially on social media to spread lies and track people. As Coeckelbergh (2022) says, "The uncritical deployment of AI systems can lead to harmful consequences, from surveillance to social manipulation." Another very big area of concern is it being used to gain unfair advantage over others especially in schools due to most artificial intelligence being capable of answering any question. This is why policies and regulation should be considered or looked at as artificial intelligence continues to pose many ethical and moral issues.
